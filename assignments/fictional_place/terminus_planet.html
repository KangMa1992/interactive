Introduction

Terminus Planet is the planet where human beings chhoose to store the all the knowledge of their civilization, so in the case that their civiliztion collapses, they can rebuilt their civilization with the knowledge stored there.

Geographical Information

Terminus Planet is a nature planet in the “Soolar System”, which is located in at the opposite of solar system.

The diameter of Terminus Planet is 24,742 kilometer, which almost equals to twice of the Earth (the planet where human beings come from, and the first planet destroyed by human beings).

Terminus revolves around the Soolar in 400 days, a period known as an Terminus year. During this time, Earth rotates about its axis about 400.25 times.

Free Entrance Policy

Terminus Planet is the territory of Republic of Galaxy, however, according to “the agreement of human civiliztion reservtion”, knowledges stored on terminus planet should be accessible to all humanbeings. Therefore, the citizens of Galaxy Empire can enter Terminus Planet with their passport with or without visa
